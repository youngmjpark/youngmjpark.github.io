Background

Megabuck Carwash Inc. is an Anaheim-based small business that provides service and space where people can wash their vehicles.

Working as a manager, I had the opportunity to design an end-to-end experience for a self-service car wash landing page, connecting physical and digital spaces.

What's the problem?

MCI. does not have a digital solution to communicate, inform and advertise to loyal but especially new car washers.


These are the problems this product aims to solve:

  • Not knowing the service pricing — There are many car wash competitors in the vicinity. Price often behaves as the initial filter for customers to decide where to wash their vehicles, but there is nowhere people can learn about price before visiting.
  • Wanting to know what is available — Car wash businesses have different tools and services unique to them. Customers want to know beforehand what is available as the specificity affects the decision (i.e. requiring air-dryer, needing big spaces).
  • Lack of on-site staff presence — MCI. is a 24-hour business which means workers are not always present and available to answer questions the customers may possess while using the service.
  • Collecting feedback and support ticket — There is no place to collect feedback and support ticket from the end user which are valuable information for business growth. Connecting with phone call is available but it is not consistent.
